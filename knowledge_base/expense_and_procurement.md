# Expense & Procurement Policy (POL-FIN-009)

## Expense Reimbursement
Submit expenses through the finance portal within 30 days of the transaction.
Receipts are required for any single expense over 25 USD. Reimbursements are paid
on the next payroll cycle after manager approval.

## Approval Thresholds
- Up to 500 USD: manager approval only.
- 500–5,000 USD: manager plus department head approval.
- Over 5,000 USD: requires a formal purchase order and Finance review.

## Software Purchases
All software and SaaS subscriptions must be requested through Procurement so that
Security can run a vendor risk review and Legal can review the contract. Personal
purchases of business software are not reimbursable.

## Travel
Book travel through the approved corporate travel tool. Economy class is standard
for flights under 6 hours; business class requires VP approval.

## Corporate Cards
Corporate cards are issued to roles with recurring spend. Lost or compromised
cards must be reported immediately to Finance, which will freeze the card and
issue a replacement.
