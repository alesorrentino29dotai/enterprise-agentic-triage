# Access Management Policy (POL-SEC-014)

## Scope
This policy governs how employees and contractors request, receive, and lose
access to internal systems at the company. It applies to all production systems,
data warehouses, and the internal admin console.

## Principle of Least Privilege
Every account is provisioned with the minimum set of permissions required for the
role. Elevated or "admin" scopes are never granted by default and always require
a documented business justification plus manager approval.

## Requesting Access
1. Submit a ticket through the IT Service Portal specifying the system, the role,
   and the business justification.
2. Standard (read-only) access to non-sensitive systems is auto-approved within
   one business day.
3. Write access, production access, and any admin scope require explicit approval
   from the resource owner and are reviewed by the Security team.

## Revocation & Offboarding
Access is revoked automatically within 4 hours of an employee's termination date
being recorded in the HR system. Quarterly access reviews remove any entitlement
that has been unused for 90 days.

## SLA
- Read-only access requests: resolved within 1 business day.
- Privileged / production access: resolved within 3 business days after approval.
- Emergency "break-glass" access: granted within 1 hour, audited within 24 hours.
